# TCP three way handshake

- Client: SYN (seq=x)        → "Can we talk? My first byte will be #x"
- Server: SYN-ACK (seq=y, ack=x+1) → "Sure! My first byte will be #y, I expect #x+1 next"
- Client: ACK (ack=y+1)      → "Great! I expect #y+1 next"
